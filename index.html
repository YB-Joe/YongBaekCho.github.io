I am a Master's Computer Science(Robotics and Artificial Intelligence) student at Arizona State University where I am a member of Active Perception Group advised by Prof. Yezhou Yang and co-advised by Prof. Yi Ren.
My research interests lie in the area of Computer Vision, Adversarial Attacks and Generative Models. My primary research focus is on how to defend or prevent the misuse of synthetic conetens from generative models under certain adversarial perturbations and robustness.
